About Us

Civil Engineering Student's Association - CESA is a departmental student's committee and has been organizing various lectures, conducting various events and carrying out projects.

The main aim of CESA is

  • To promote the study of Civil and Environmental Engineering
  • To provide civil engineering students an opportunity to connect with their peers for sharing knowledge
  • To shape the students future

CESA organizes National level competitions and many practical-oriented civil engineering workshops. Every year CESA organizes a national level technical fest 'STHAPATYA' for the students of various colleges from the civil engineering discipline, that includes competitions such as Bridge-It, Town Planning, CAD-WAR, Miniature Modelling and many such exclusive competitions and workshops.

About VJTI

The Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute was established in 1887. Since the time of inception, VJTI has been playing a very vital role in producing quality engineers, introducing new programmes, and electives in emerging areas. The institute was granted financial and academic autonomy from June 21, 2004. The institute is reputed for excellent training in Engineering and Technology at Diploma, Degree, Post Graduate levels and Research. Presently VJTI offers B.Tech programmes in nine core disciplines of engineering, 17 M.Tech programmes, M.C.A and PhD programmes in various engineering disciplines.

Guest Lectures

World's Largest Stadium - Narendra Modi Stadium by Mr. Subrata Dutta

Mr. Subrata Dutta is the Project Director of Narendra Modi Stadium. Narendra Modi Stadium is the largest stadium in the world. Also known as the Motera, after the place where it is located. Built on the banks of the Sabarmati river on the outskirts of Ahmedabad, the Sardar Patel Stadium is an imposing structure which can accommodate about 54,000 spectators. The stadium was built on a 50-acre land donated by the state government, and took only nine months for its completion

World's Tallest Statue - Statue of Unity by Mr. Mukesh Raval

Mr. Mukesh Raval is the Project Director of the Statue of Unity. Statue of Unity is the world’s tallest statue at 182m height, located in Gujarat. The Statue of Unity is a statue of Indian statesman and independence activist Vallabhbhai Patel, who was the first deputy prime minister and home minister of independent India. Costed nearly ₹3,000 crore rupees to build it.

The Restoration Man of India - Mr. Chetan Raikar

Mr. Chetan Raikar is the Restoration Man of India. His works include the Hotel Taj restoration after the 26/11 attack. He is the Chairman and Managing Director at Structwel Designers & Consultants Pvt. Lmt. He spoke about various restoration projects in India and abroad.

Career Prospects in Civil Engineering

We had an interactive session with four alumni from the batch of 1998, all now well settled now and residing abroad. They discussed about how Civil Engineering Societies abroad are different from ours and what all skills should we be learning to succeed in our careers as Civil Engineers.

Inauguration by Minister of Water Resources of Maharashtra

We had the privilege to have Mr. Jayant Patil, Minister of Water Resources of Maharashtra and also former VJTI student, as our chief guess during STHAPATYA 2020. He also was the inaugurator of the event. In his speech, Mr. Jayant Patil stated how impressed he was by all the innovative events organized and the concepts behind them. He also stated how glad he was to accept our invitation and that he could witness the fest and our events live.

Understanding Entrepreneurship

During our Entrepreneurship Week 2021, we had provided young enthusiasts with an opportunity to learn the ways of entrepreneurship from a personality who had herself reached greater heights in Civil industry by nurturing the leader within her. Priyanka Gupta, the founder of Rezovate Constructions, gave us a talk on how she got into Civil industry as an entrepreneur inspite of not having any Civil Engineering background and why inspired her to do so.

AutoCAD Workshop

AutoCAD is an essential tool required to make plans for buildings or other structures. CESA VJTI organized a 2 hour free workshop on 19th February for Architecture and Civil Engineering students. It had participation from over 170+ students. It was an engaging and interactive session conducted by Mr. Anjaneya Puli, who has 12+ years of experience of teaching Civil softwares

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I have to come to VJTI in order to participate in any of the events?

    In awake of the ongoing pandemic, CESA, VJTI has decided to go online and hence we will be holding STHAPATYA 2021 online and you can participate in any of the events from comfort of your home.

  • How do I get in contact with committee for other matters and queries?

    You can click on the 'contact' tab or scroll to the bottom of the page and put down your concern or queries in there. We'll reach out to you ASAP.

  • What if I have a doubt regarding any of the events?

    Go to the contact section at the bottom of this page and write us a message

  • How do I pay the registration fees?

    Well, you're in for a treat. All the events are totally FREE.

  • How to register for the events?

    Open the event tab and go through the rules and regulations of the event. Then click on the register button and register for the event. For events where you have to participate in teams, state one team head with whom communication will happen. And then list remaining team members.

  • What is the last date for registration of events?

    There is no "last date" as such. (Of course before the events xD) But, the registrations will be closed as soon as we reach our upper limit per event. So, hurry up!
